Welcome to Sky High Quiz!

This quick guide will ensure you have a great experience.

Getting Started

Choose "New Player" and enter your username to begin. Follow the on-screen prompts to navigate through the game. The game features automatic background music. You have the option to toggle the sound on or off at any time using the "Sound On/Off" button found in the game menu.

Gameplay Overview

Your goal is to correctly answer trivia questions to earn points. Look for a blue marker on the map. This single blue marker indicates a trivia question.

Question Interaction

If you answer correctly, you'll be rewarded with a colorful confetti explosion. An incorrect answer will display a red cross icon. After answering a question, this blue marker turns red (indicating the question has been attempted) and displays a message, signaling that you should move on. Click the "Next" button in the menu to find your next single blue marker (i.e, your next trivia question).

Answering Questions

A map marker corresponds to a trivia question. Click on it to view the question. Choose your answer from the given multiple-choice options. Correct answers will earn you 500 points. There's no penalty for wrong answers.

Scoring System

Your current score is displayed in the top left corner of the screen. Try to achieve the highest possible score before the time runs out.

Time Limit

You have a total of 3 minutes to play the game. A warning alert will notify you when only 5 seconds remain. Use this time wisely!

Pausing and Quitting the Game

You can pause the game at any moment by clicking the "Pause" button, which temporarily stops the timer. To resume, click on "Continue" found in the pause window that appears on clicking the "Pause" button in the menu. If you decide to quit, the "Quit Game" button will redirect you back to the login screen.

End of the Game

When the timer expires, the game ends, and you'll see a popup displaying your final score along with a message based on your performance. To start a new game, simply go through the login process i.e, the same steps listed from the beginning of this page.

Additional Tips

Be curious and explore the map to discover a new trivia question every time. Keep an eye on both the timer and your score to strategize effectively.
